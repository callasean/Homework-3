Homework #3

You have a web business selling star wars toys, you get them from a supplier and they aren't quite approved toys so they can't be called star wars, but they are plainly drawn from the same universe. They seem to appeal most to pre-teen boys, but you have a significant secondary following among gamers of all genders since the characters have found their way into an online role playing game.

Web Marketing Plan

  1. Choose ideal customer, decide on demographic
  2. Create Facebook, Twitter, Instagram Accounts and post relevant material that attracts attention
  3. Create SEO articles, using keywords related to product
  4. Create a mailing list section on website where customers can sign up for emails from the company about new products and promotions
  5. Look for other websites that are in the same market, buy ad space and communicate brand through images/ videos
  6. Create Google analytics account to track campaigns, adjust strategies to what works and what doesn't
